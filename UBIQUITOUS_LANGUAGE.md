## Maintenance rules

- Put new terms in the most specific owning file.
- You are allowed to create a new file in `docs/ubiquitous-language` if the term does not fit in any of the categories
- Do not redefine a term in multiple files.
- Use links when one bounded context refers to another.
- Keep definitions to one sentence.
